  • About
  • Upgrades helps you to improve your whole business. With them, you can increase the money you win when you shoot, the cost of different drugs, etc.
    • Builds are used to create and stock drugs each second.

  • What is "prestige"?
    Your prestige rank is determined by your experience. You win experience in Blackmarket by earning cash.
    To have a prestige rank, you'll have to soft-reset your game. It seems that you lose everything except totals and, obviously, experience.
