Welcome to Idle-Quest!
This game is a remake of the original Android game.

As your player gain experience (XP), your player will level-up. It's the base mechanic of the game. Your player level determine the difficulty of the quests.
Your player got a sword, boots and an amulet. Each piece can be upgraded in the 'craft' menu. You can craft better pieces by using a certain % of your gold and spending some levels.

Attack (so your sword damage) impacts how quickly you will complete 'attack quests'.
Speed (so your boots speed) impacts how quickly you will complete 'speed quests'.
Amulet (so your amulet luck) impacts the gold reward of each quests.

The game runs also when it's closed. So don't worry about this, but you can always active/disable 'auto-idle'.

About the prestige system, it will consume all your levels and gold, and reset all your equipment. It's recommended to prestige as soon as possible!
